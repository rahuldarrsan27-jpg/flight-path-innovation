SUSTAINABILITY

Responsible operations,
by design

Extending airframe life is inherently sustainable work. We pair that with cleaner ground operations, careful resource stewardship and transparent compliance — the standards lessors and airlines increasingly expect of their MRO partners.

OUR COMMITMENTS

Four pillars of responsible MRO

Cleaner ground operations

Reducing emissions and noise on the ramp and in the hangar through smarter ground support and energy use.

  • Fixed electrical ground power over APU running
  • Transition toward electric & low-emission GSE
  • SAF-ready handling & fuelling processes

Resource stewardship

Managing the by-products of heavy maintenance responsibly, from effluent to consumables.

  • Controlled effluent & chemical handling
  • Recycling of metals, packaging & consumables
  • Water & solvent reduction programs

Responsible asset preservation

Proper storage and preservation protects airframe value and avoids premature retirement and waste.

  • Lifecycle-extending preservation programs
  • Corrosion control reducing structural waste
  • Return-to-service readiness over scrappage

Transparency & compliance

Operating to documented standards and reporting that withstand lessor and airline vendor audits.

  • GCAA & environmental directive alignment
  • Auditable processes & record-keeping
  • Continuous improvement targets

OUR PLEDGE

Every airframe we keep airworthy is an asset that flies longer, ferries less, and retires later — sustainability and engineering excellence, working as one.
